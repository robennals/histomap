Features

🔍

Interactive Exploration

Click on any event to learn more via Wikipedia. Zoom, pan, and customize your view.

📊

World Power Visualization

See how economic power shifted between nations and blocs over time with GDP data from the Maddison Project.

📄

PDF Export

Generate high-quality PDFs of your timeline for printing or sharing.

🛠

Customizable Layers

Toggle event categories on and off. Show only what interests you: wars, technology, culture, and more.

Powered by Real Data

World power GDP data comes from the Maddison Project Database, a comprehensive source for historical economic development data spanning millennia.
